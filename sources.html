Inigo Quilez

Inigo is a graphics programmer that has contributed greatly to the world of computer graphics. He co-created shadertoy, an amazing website for finding and learning shaders in webgl. His website is filled with educational tutorials and cool projects he's worked on over the years.

Book of Shaders

Priceless resource for getting into shaders and understanding some more of the foundations of computer art. Goes from the very basics all the way up to fractal brownian motion. It has been left in an unfinished state for years which is a huge bummer, but the chapters that are available are great. Explains things very incrementaly that doesn't feel overwhelming.

David Daniels

Inventor of Strata Cut, a technique that reconstructs the 3rd dimension of claymation to a time dimension. We see a 2 dimensional image that warps over time as fine cuts are taken from a textured or animated block of clay.
